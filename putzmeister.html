Putzmeister:

Putzmeister develops, produces and sells machines not only for pumping and distributing concrete, mortar and high-density solids but also for preparing, temporarily storing and processing these materials: Placing of concrete, Truck-mounted and stationary concrete pumps, stationary placing booms and accessories, the mobile belt conveyor Telebelt® as well as professional consulting in large construction projects. The fields of application for our machines are civil engineering, mining and tunnelling and pre cast factories as well as large-scale industrial projects and power stations.
